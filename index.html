To prevent the risk of having a space suspended by the multi-billion company that discord is, one must understand the root of the problem. The elitists have begun relying on a symmetric block cipher algorithm with a block/chunk size of 256 bits in order to encrypt lolicon illustrations by using open-source code either via extension, plugin, windows shell or userscript. For functionality & authentification, keys must be exchanged between the recipients through a cryptographic key exchange algorithm. In our case, the protocol Elliptic Curve Diffie-Hellman P-521 is used, and the key exchange takes place in the direct messages of the recipient. Since discord does not have access to the key, they are therefore unable to decrypt said content especially since it is almost impossible to break through such tight encryption. Even if their Trust & Safety team were to obtain said key, there has not been a single case where they have used the provided key to decrypt the content.
